About ETHOS

ETHOS is an observatory on public ethics founded by Sebastiano Maffettone at LUISS Guido Carli University in Rome. The Alumni Network brings together the ETHOS people — students, i ragazzi di Young ETHOS, alumni, and collaborators — with the goal of maintaining connections and creating long-term value and social capital.

Members come from diverse academic and professional backgrounds — from political philosophy and international relations to AI ethics, sustainability, and business ethics — united by a shared commitment to rigorous thinking about the public good.

"Ethics is not merely an academic discipline — it is a civic obligation. The ETHOS network exists to keep that obligation alive across careers, borders, and generations."
Prof. Sebastiano Maffettone, Founding Director
